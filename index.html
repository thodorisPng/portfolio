Case Study – Social Networking App for Musicians

Problem Statement

Many amateur musicians struggle to connect with like-minded performers for collaborations, practice sessions, or gigs. The goal was to design an app that makes networking seamless, intuitive, and engaging.

Research Findings

Conducted interviews with 15 musicians and surveyed 50+ users to identify pain points: difficulty finding collaborators, unclear profiles, and fragmented communication tools.
